UCA Data Visualizations 2016 - 2017

The following data visualizatons were created by the UCA Student Life Office using the D3 Javascript library.

Demographic Dashboard

Basic demographic dashboard of broken down by countries, majors, gender, rural/urban.

Intended Majors

This Sankey Diagram was shows the flow of majors over time. Beginning of year -> midyear -> after summer semester.

Network Graph

This social network graph was generated by asking students who they knew prior to being admitted and coming to UCA. Potential application: targeted social media campaigns.

Correlation Matrix

This correlation matrix shows the relationship between admissions test scores and first semester academic performance.
