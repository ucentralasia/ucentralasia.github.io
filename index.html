UCA Data Visualizations 2016 - 2017

The following data visualizatons were created by the UCA Student Life Office using the D3 Javascript library.

Demographic Dashboard

Basic demographic dashboard of students broken down by countries, majors, gender, rural-urban.

Intended Majors

This Sankey diagram shows the flow of intended majors of students over time. Beginning of year -> midyear -> after summer semester (to be updated).

Network Graph

This social network graph was generated by asking students who they knew prior to being admitted and coming to UCA, in order to understand pre-existing social networks.

Correlation Matrix

This correlation matrix shows the relationship between 2016 admissions test scores and first semester academic performance of students.
